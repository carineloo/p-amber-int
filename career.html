Accounts & Admin Assistant
(Full Time)

Join us and accomplish great things in a global company that’s shaping the future of Investments.

Principal Duties and Responsibilities
  • Assist in preparing full set accounts and admin matters.
  • Perform checking and verifying all the documents.
  • Perform bookkeeping duties including data entries, updating & maintaining debtor and creditor aging, invoicing, receipts, etc.
  • Ensure all schedule payments are paid on time in accordance with company’s guidelines.
  • Assist with the month end closing related to monthly management report, bank reconciliation and other supporting schedules and worksheet.
  • Assist and handle any ad-hoc assignment as and when it’s required.
Job Requirements
  • At least 5 years of working experience in the related field.
  • Candidate must possess at least a Professional Certificate, Diploma or equivalent.
Required Skills
  • Ms Word, Ms Excel
  • Initiative, trusworthy, self-motivated, independent.
