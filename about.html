DIRECTOR / CHIEF EXECUTIVE OFFICER

NUR ARINA AZREEN BINTI ABDUL BASHER

Nur Arina Azreen Binti Abdul Basher (“Reena Basher”) is the Chief Executive Officer and major shareholder of Precious Amber International Berhad. She has vast experience in financial planning, investment analysis and business development. Backed by a Certificate in Accounting and numerous financial accolades, Reena Basher has built a decade of record in planning investment strategies and developing financial plans for businesses and individuals.

Reena Basher has been accredited the Federation of Investment Managers Malaysia (“FIMM”) license, Futures Trading Certificate (Jakarta Futures Exchange - JFX), Pre-Contract Examination for Insurance Agent (“PCEIA”), Certificate Examination in Investment-Linked Life Insurance (“CEILI”), Basic Certificate Course in Takaful Broking (“BCCITB”) and Certificate in Private Retirement Scheme (“PRS”). In addition, she has been awarded Top Equity for Loco London Market (JFX) in 2012, Regional Top Rookie Award in 2013, Platinum Black Premier Consultant in 2014, Top Personal Sale Equity Market in 2015, Million Dollar Round Table (2014, 2015 and 2016) and Excellent Award for Agency Leader-Bumiputera in 2017.

In March 2020, Reena Basher completed the Oxford Entrepreneurship - Venture Finance Program, developed by Said Business School, University of Oxford, UK. In March 2021, she obtained the Certificate of CFE (Certified Fraud Examiner) from the Association of Certified Fraud Examiners (ACFE).

On 18 March 2021, Reena Basher received the Awards for “Top young CEO - Women in Business Industry” and “Top Young CEO - Financial Institution Industry” from the Top 100 Young CEO Awards 2020 organised by IKON Malaysia.

Our Story

What We Do & How We Got Here

Precious Amber International invests in businesses with cross-border strategy and act as financial advisor for our clients. We focus into high-growth industries, which include technology, health-care, renewable energy and properties. We are partners with Licensed Financial Institutions for their network and research, while also working with global insurance companies for insurance protection and provide comfort and security to our clients.
