DIRECTOR / CHIEF EXECUTIVE OFFICER

HAJJAH NUR ARINA AZREEN BINTI HAJI ABDUL BASHER

Hajjah Nur Arina Azreen Binti Haji Abdul Basher ("Nur Arina"), an esteemed Malaysian professional, holds a majority stake of 52.41% in the ordinary shares of the Company. She is the co-founder and Chief Executive Officer of Precious Amber. Her distinguished career is underscored by a robust portfolio encompassing financial planning, investment analysis, and business development, establishing her as an eminent figure within her field.

Underpinned by a Certificate in Accounting and a string of esteemed Financial Accolades, Nur Arina's legacy is defined by over a decade of proficiency in architecting astute Investment Strategies and curating bespoke financial frameworks for both corporate entities and individuals. Her unwavering dedication to excellence was evidenced by her triumphant completion of the revered Oxford Entrepreneurship - Venture Finance Program in March 2020, a milestone achieved through Said Business School at the University of Oxford, UK. Moreover, her quest for knowledge extended to her participation in the corporate finance and strategy program at the prestigious London School of Economics in 2022.

Nur Arina's distinguished qualifications are further amplified by a spectrum of industry-recognized certifications. Notably, she holds a Federation of Investment Managers, Malaysia (FIMM) license, a Futures Trading Certificate from the Jakarta Futures Exchange (JFX), and a constellation of achievements, including the Pre-Contract Examination for Insurance Agent (PCEIA), Certificate Examination in Investment-Linked Life Insurance (CEILI), Basic Certificate Course in Takaful Broking (BCCITB), and Certificate in Private Retirement Scheme (PRS).

Her trajectory is punctuated by a constellation of accolades, including the Top Equity for Loco London Market (JFX) award in 2012, the prestigious Regional Top Rookie Award in 2013, the revered Platinum Black Premier Consultant recognition in 2014, and her standing as a Top Personal Sale Equity Market achiever in 2015. Her persistent ascent is underscored by her inclusion in the elite Million Dollar Round Table in 2014, 2015, and 2016, alongside her distinction as the Excellent Award Winner for Agency Leader-Bumiputera recipient in 2017.

Nur Arina's resolute pursuit of excellence continues to reap accolades. Notably, she attained the esteemed Certified Fraud Examiner (CFE) certification, awarded by the globally acclaimed Association of Certified Fraud Examiners (ACFE). This achievement was further underscored by her recognition in the esteemed Top 100 Young CEO Awards 2020, organized by IKON Malaysia, wherein she garnered recognition for her exceptional accomplishments in the "Top young CEO - Women in Business Industry" and "Top Young CEO - Financial Institution Industry" domains in March 2021.
